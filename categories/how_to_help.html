Luckily, you can play your part in beating cancer right now. There are many ways to get involved.



Take part in an event



Take on a challenge and have fun, all while raising vital funds for cancer research. We have events across the UK to suit everyone, including cycling, jogging, hiking and more.


Volunteer with us



Our volunteers save lives. And even though things are a bit different at the moment, we need dedicated volunteers more than ever. There are still so many ways to support our life-saving work.


Pledge to leave a gift in your will



Make a lasting legacy by leaving a gift in your will.  Your pledge means we can make even greater leaps forward in our life-saving research to help beat cancer for future generations.


Donate to support our work



Your donations support research that saves lives. A regular income means we can plan ahead, committing to long-term research projects with huge potential that could help save more lives.


Do your own fundraising



Whether you're looking for ideas, tips or materials, we've got everything you need to help make your fundraising a success.


Campaign for change



Cancer is political. Campaigning saves lives. Campaign to prevent, treat and diagnose cancer through influencing your politicians and local communities.


Together, we will beat cancer
